Old Age Home Visit

A Day of Sharing, Caring, and Connection

Our Heartfelt Initiative

On 7th January 2025, the Rotaract Clubs of NLJIET and LDCE united for a heartwarming visit to a local old age home. This initiative was more than an event; it was a mission to spread love, combat loneliness, and honor the wisdom of our elders, making them feel truly cherished and valued.

A Day to Remember

Heartfelt Conversations

We began the day by listening to incredible life stories and sharing moments of laughter, bridging generations.

Joyful Games & Garba

The atmosphere buzzed with energy during musical chairs, passing the parcel, and an enthusiastic round of Garba.

Kindness in Action

We distributed food packets as a small gesture of our care, which was met with the warmest smiles and blessings.

Aarti & Unity

The visit concluded with a divine aarti ceremony, creating a powerful sense of peace and togetherness.

Moments of Joy

Music & Dance

Celebrating life and culture through the universal language of music and dance.

Service & Fellowship

Fulfilling the core Rotaract values of selfless service and building strong community bonds.

Spreading Happiness

Witnessing pure joy and creating lasting memories was the biggest reward for everyone involved.
