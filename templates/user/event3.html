Khushiyon Ka Sangam

Fellowship at Sabarmati Riverfront

A Riverside Retreat

On 9th February 2025, we gathered at the serene Sabarmati Riverfront for an unforgettable fellowship, strengthening bonds and creating cherished memories.

Event Flow

Energetic Gaming Session

The day kicked off with lively games like Ubhi-Kho, Dumb Charades, and Badminton, filling the air with laughter.

Surya Namaskar Break

A rejuvenating wellness session in the middle of the event to refresh our minds and bodies by the riverside.

Continued Fellowship

The enthusiasm continued with more games and interaction, strengthening the bonds of fellowship among all members.

Joyful Conclusion

The event wrapped up with a profound sense of unity and joy, leaving everyone with lasting memories.

Fun & Fellowship

Traditional Games

Members enjoyed classic Indian games like Ubhi-Kho, bringing a nostalgic and competitive flair.

Dumb Charades

A hilarious session of creative acting and guessing that had everyone in splits.

Wellness Moment

The Surya Namaskar activity promoted physical and mental well-being among participants.

Friendly Competition

From UNO to Badminton, the spirit of friendly rivalry kept the energy high throughout the day.
