Blood Donation Camp

Give the Gift of Life

A Commitment to Humanity

On 31st January 2025, our club organized a Blood Donation Camp, uniting students and faculty in a powerful act of social responsibility to save lives.

The Donation Journey

Awareness & Guidance

The camp began with an informative session by medical experts, dispelling myths and encouraging participation.

The Life-Saving Act

Donors enthusiastically stepped forward to donate under safe, hygienic, and professional medical supervision.

Care & Appreciation

Post-donation, every hero was provided with refreshments and a certificate of appreciation for their noble act.

A Community of Givers

The event saw an overwhelming response, fostering a culture of compassion and giving within our community.

Why It Matters

Saving Lives

Every single unit of blood collected has the potential to save up to three lives in critical need.

Safe & Professional

Our camp was conducted with a certified blood bank, ensuring the highest standards of safety and hygiene.

Community Spirit

This event showcased the incredible power of our community coming together for a vital cause.
